The Renal cell carcinoma (RCC) denotes cancer originated from the renal epithelium and accounts for >90% of cancers in the kidney. The major established risk factors for RCC include excess body weight, hypertension and cigarette smoking.

  •  It is critical to have reliable techniques at predicting RCC at early stages
  • Machine Learning (ML) techniques are excellent in predicting RCC.
  • The current study offers a methodology for predicting RCC status using clinical data, which incorporates data pre-processing, a technique for managing missing values, data aggregation, and feature extraction. ML techniques such as decision tree (DT) classification and random forest classifier , were used in this work to train model for reliable prediction.
